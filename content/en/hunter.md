### Hallucination and Bias Hunter

We live in a world where reality and technology influence each other in ways that can be hard to comprehend. It's worth asking a few questions: Which would leave a bigger digital footprint, a peaceful demonstration or one that turns violent? An outrageous statement or a balanced one?

Understanding the limitations and weaknesses of our brain is crucial, but it's equally important to grasp the constraints of technology. Language models are trained on data from the Internet and might reflect biases present in the training data, but they can also introduce their own, resulting from probability optimization or a lack of context understanding.
